== Description ==
Regina Lite is a medical WordPress theme, suitable for medical clinics, dentistry websites, medical research facilities and more. If you were to categorise Regina Lite, you'd have to say it's one of the best health WordPress themes. Or that's what we strongly believe.
If you're looking for health care and medical WordPress themes, Regina Lite is your best bet. Get the best experience a WordPress theme built for doctors, by doctors, has to offer. Pick one of the best WordPress medical templates on the market - Regina Lite.
